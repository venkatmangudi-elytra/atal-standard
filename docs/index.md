# ATAL – AI Traceability & Accountability Ledger  

## Public Review Draft v0.9 – Coming 25 December

This is the pre-release information site for **ATAL (AI Traceability & Accountability Ledger)**, a vendor-neutral, implementation-independent standard for AI traceability, auditability, and autonomy governance.

The **full public review draft (v0.9)** – including the complete specification and governance documents – will be published here on:

**25 December**

On that date, the global community will be invited to review and comment on the standard through the public GitHub repository.

---

## What you can see for now

Ahead of the full v0.9 preview, a small set of orientation documents is available:

- **[About ATAL](ABOUT_ATAL.md)**  
  High-level purpose, scope, and regulatory alignment.

- **[ATAL Primer](ATAL_Primer.md)**  
  A concise overview of the nine Parts of the standard.

- **[ATAL FAQ](FAQ.md)**  
  Common questions about scope, usage, and governance.

These documents are informational and do not yet open the formal public review window.

---

## Public review window

On **25 December**, the following will go live:

- Full ATAL Specification v0.9  
- Governance and stewardship documentation  
- Public review process and timelines  

Feedback will be accepted via **GitHub Issues** in the public ATAL repository.
